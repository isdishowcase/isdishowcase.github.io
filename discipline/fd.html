Dhruv

THE XCLUSIVELY HYPERTHERMIC JOHN DOE

"Everything in my path has brought me to this city and the woman at its helm. In honor of my future-nostalgia, I have envisioned a city surreal, where time will never erase the urban scenery we hold tight, her uniform unabashedly anachronistic street style. I call it “My Hyperthermic City”, one where human cryonic preservation and reanimation is commercially possible. Immortality rules the tonality and mood of this city, where my woman finds herself re-born, chasing her fierce passion for the 90s, the city skyline keeping us hyperthermic. She is an urban punk warrior. We battle the consequent crimes in this city and embrace our re-birth, youthfully making the city our own shades of blurred melodrama. This is our city, this is our nostalgia, this is my future lens for me and my woman."
